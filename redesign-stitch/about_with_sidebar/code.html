I build tools for the curious.

Based in Berlin, I spend my time crafting iOS experiences that feel like they belong. I believe software should be as intentional as a physical object.

My journey started with a fascination for typography and industrial design, which eventually led me to the world of Swift and Human Interface Guidelines. Today, I balance my time between independent app development and consulting for startups that value aesthetic precision.

When I'm not pushing pixels or writing typesafe code, you'll find me at a local espresso bar or hiking through the Black Forest with a film camera in hand.
